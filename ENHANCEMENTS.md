# Future Enhancements

Ideas for future Big Surf Workout Tracker improvements.

---

## 1. Equipment Manager Page

A dedicated page for managing gym equipment inventory.

**Features:**
- View all saved equipment across locations
- Add/edit/delete equipment
- Associate equipment with specific gym locations
- Equipment notes and settings preferences
- Quick search/filter by location or equipment type

---

## 2. Smart Workouts / DEXA Integration

Integration with DEXA body composition scans to provide intelligent workout recommendations.

**Features:**
- Import DEXA scan results (muscle mass distribution, fat distribution)
- Identify lagging muscle groups based on scan data
- Auto-generate workout recommendations targeting weak points
- Track body composition changes over time
- Correlate workout history with composition improvements

---

## 3. Equipment-Based Plan Builder

Build workout plans based on available equipment at a specific location.

**Features:**
- Select a gym/location to see available equipment
- Auto-suggest exercises based on equipment inventory
- Build custom workout templates constrained by equipment
- "What can I do today?" feature based on current location
- Handle equipment availability (in-use/out-of-order tracking)

---

## 4. Community Gym Equipment Database

Crowdsourced database of gym equipment configurations.

**Features:**
- Users can contribute equipment lists for public gyms
- Search gyms by name/location to pre-populate equipment
- Rating/verification system for community contributions
- "Find gyms with X equipment" search
- Sync community data with personal equipment lists
- Privacy controls for personal gym setups

---
