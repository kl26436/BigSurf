# Big Surf privacy policy

**Effective date: [DATE — set at launch]**

Big Surf is a workout tracker built by [YOUR NAME / ENTITY] ("we," "us"). This policy explains what data the app collects, why, and what control you have over it. The short version: your training data is yours, we don't sell it, we don't show ads, and you can delete everything whenever you want.

## What we collect

**Account information.** You sign in with Google. We receive your name, email address, and Google account ID. We don't see your Google password.

**Workout data.** The workouts you log: exercises, sets, reps, weights, rest times, notes, workout duration, and dates. This is the core of the app and it's stored so you can see your history and progress.

**Body measurements (optional).** If you use these features: body weight, body measurements, and DEXA scan results. If you upload a DEXA scan PDF, we store the file and the body-composition numbers extracted from it. You can delete any of these at any time.

**Withings (optional).** If you connect a Withings account, we sync your body weight from Withings. You can disconnect at any time in Settings, which stops the sync.

**Location (optional).** If you grant location permission, the app uses GPS when you start a workout to detect which of your saved gyms you're at. We store the gyms you save (name and coordinates) and the gym name on each workout. We do not track your location in the background or outside of this feature. You can deny location permission and pick your gym manually instead.

**Equipment and gym libraries.** The gyms and equipment you set up in the app.

**Push notifications (optional).** If you enable rest-timer notifications, we store a notification subscription token for your device.

**Diagnostics.** Basic error logs when something in the app breaks, so we can fix it. These are not sold or used for advertising.

## How the AI features work

If you use the AI Coach or DEXA scan import, relevant data is sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) to generate the response: for the Coach, a summary of your training history and your question; for DEXA import, the PDF you upload. Under our agreement with the provider, this data is not used to train their models. If you don't use these features, nothing is sent.

## Where your data lives

Data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Cloud Storage) in the United States, protected by authentication and per-user access rules — your data is readable only by your signed-in account. Data is encrypted in transit.

## What we don't do

- We don't sell your data. To anyone. Ever.
- We don't show ads or share data with advertisers.
- We don't track you across other apps or websites.

## Data retention and deletion

Your data is kept as long as you have an account. You can delete individual workouts, measurements, and scans in the app at any time.

To delete your entire account and all associated data, use **Settings → Delete account** in the app, or email us at [SUPPORT EMAIL]. Deletion removes your workouts, measurements, DEXA files, gyms, equipment, preferences, and account record. This is permanent.

You can export your data (JSON/CSV) from Settings before deleting.

## Children

Big Surf is not directed at children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect data from them. If you believe a child under 13 has an account, contact us and we'll delete it.

## Changes to this policy

If we change this policy in a way that matters, we'll note it in the app and update the date at the top. Continued use after a change means you accept the updated policy.
