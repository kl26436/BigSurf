Updated dashboard composition

New section names:

  • Body Parts → Training — how you're training each muscle group (or alt: "Muscles", "Lifts", "Strength")
  • Body → Composition — what your body is made of (donut, weight trend)

New section order (top to bottom):

  1. Greeting + avatar
  2. Active workout pill (conditional)
  3. 3-chip hero row (Streak · Week · Body weight)
  4. Insight card (moved up — actionable, demands attention)
  5. For [today] (moved up — your fastest path to starting)
  6. Training (6 muscle-group cards)
  7. Composition (donut + weight)
  8. Recent PRs

3-level navigation:

  • Level 1: Dashboard (summary)
  • Level 2: Muscle group detail (tap Chest card) — per-exercise breakdown
  • Level 3: Exercise detail (tap Bench Press row inside Chest) — 1RM trend, best sets, every session, form video, equipment, notes
