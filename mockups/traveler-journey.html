Traveler journey — Tier 3

The trip so far (tiers 0–2, shipped): land at a hotel gym → GPS misses → name "Marriott Austin" → quick-add 6 machines from the catalog in ~2 minutes. Tier 3 is the payoff: the app now tells you what you can do here (3.1) and remembers your machine settings (3.2). Companion doc: docs/traveler-flow.md.

Step 0 — First visit, workout-first

Before any of the planner magic: the gym has nothing mapped. Nobody stocks a gym before leg day — the gym gets mapped as a byproduct of the workout. Until the community gym DB exists, this is also every brand-new user's first session, so these four screens are the activation path.

3.1 — "What can I do here?"

Workouts page gains a gym context chip, compatibility badges, and a "Possible here" filter. Badges only render when at least one workout isn't fully possible — at your home gym the page looks exactly like today. Positive claims only: zero matches reads "Not mapped here yet," never "Not possible" — the app can't know what it hasn't seen.

Step 3 — Badges
Workouts page at the travel gym
Gym chip shows the detected location. Every card gets a badge — green and warm chips claim only what's mapped; unmapped workouts get a neutral invitation, not a verdict.
